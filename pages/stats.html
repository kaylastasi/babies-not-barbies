STATS

By children competing in these events, they are taught at a very early age that their only value comes from their appearance and the way they look. These children end up concentrating too much on their external looks rather than developing internal values. Unfortunately, this can lead to children feeling defeated if they grow up and not feeling as if they look good enough. From childhood, these young girls are faced with the struggles of perfection which can lead to dieting, eating disorders, low self esteem, body issues, and even depression. These issues for children can possibly lead into their adulthood.
